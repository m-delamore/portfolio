DigiPen Institute Nostalgia

Spotlights for a few of my best DigiPen projects from way back while I was pursuing my Bachelor's in Game Design. I greatly missed the satisfaction of completing projects when I'm involved in the creation process.

*Note that I have officially changed my surname in 2016. The names you would see is my old name.

Iris

A 2D platformer, with archery mechanic and wonderful cinematic story.

This game was developed by a team of 4 Digipen students, including myself, with the use of the game editor Unity, and other tools to help with animation and analytics. Music was provided by our esteemed lecturer who was also an accomplished audio engineer.

The coolest thing we've done in this project was the cinematics that conveyed the story of twin siblings and the struggle to save each other.

It won the Excellent Student Award at IGF China 2014.

Omega Chronicles: Coming of Cimmerian

A 90-page table-top RPG rulebook I created from scratch. It took me about 3 months to complete. It could be an RPG 3rd-person game on its own with different classes to choose from and players can create their own avatar, though it lacks details about the technical implementation.

The Witcher - Visenna's Legacy DLC

The Witcher had fascinated me at that point of time. I decided to choose this game as part of the game design assignment. I created a design document constructing the general paths for a DLC to The Witcher game.

More About Myself

I graduated from DigiPen with a degree in Game Design back in 2014. My first job in the gaming industry was as a Unity Developer for a company that makes mobile games. More interested in eventually landing a Game Designer role, I switched to testing games and had thought at that time to slowly step up from there.


Well, almost a decade later and that did not happened. So I'm back at developing games instead. This time, I set my ambition to work in a AAA game studio that makes awesome games.
